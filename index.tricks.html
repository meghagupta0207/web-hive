Do A Barrel Roll

Typing this into the Google search bar makes the screen perform a 360-degree spin.

Google Gravity

Search for "Google Gravity" and click on the "I'm Feeling Lucky" button to see a playful interactive animation where the Google homepage falls apart.

Google Askew

Searching for "askew" tilts the search results page slightly.

Google Zerg Rush

Typing "Zerg Rush" causes small "O" characters to attack your search results, which you can "defeat" by clicking them.

Google Thanos Snap

Search for "Thanos" and click the Infinity Gauntlet icon (on supported devices). It snaps half of your search results away

Google HTML Blink

Type "Blink HTML" into Google, and all the instances of "blink" on the page will blink.

Google In 1998

This search takes you back to what Google’s interface looked like in 1998.

Google Anagram

When you search for "Anagram," Google cheekily asks, "Did you mean: nag a ram?"—which is itself an anagram.

Google Coin Flip

Type these to use Google's virtual coin-flipping.

Google Spinner

Searching for "Fidget Spinner" brings up an interactive spinner you can use to pass time.
